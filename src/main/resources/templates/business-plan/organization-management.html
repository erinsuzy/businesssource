Organization and Management

Describe how your business is organized and who’s involved. You might include:

  • Ownership structure (e.g., sole proprietorship, LLC, partnership)
  • Your role in the business
  • Key team members and their responsibilities
  • Advisors, mentors, or external professionals (e.g., accountant, attorney)

Not sure how to describe your structure? Here’s a quick guide:

  • Sole Proprietorship – You’re the only owner and fully responsible. Easiest to set up, but no legal separation between you and the business.
  • LLC (Limited Liability Company) – More formal. Offers legal protection by separating your personal assets from the business. May have one or more owners.

You don’t need to choose right now — just describe how you plan to run things for now.
