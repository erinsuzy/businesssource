Funding Request

If you need money to start or grow your business, describe how much you need, what you'll use it for, and how you plan to get it. You might include:

  • How much funding you’re requesting (if any)
  • What the funds will be used for (e.g., equipment, insurance, website, marketing)
  • How you plan to obtain the funding (e.g., personal savings, loan, grant, crowdfunding)
  • Whether you’ll seek outside investors or remain self-funded

*If you don’t plan to request funding, feel free to note that here.*
