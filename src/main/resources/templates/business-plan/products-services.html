Products and Services

Describe what your business offers. You can include:

  • The services or products you plan to offer
  • How they meet the needs of your target market
  • What makes them unique or valuable
  • Your pricing strategy (e.g., hourly, flat rate, package deals)
  • How your prices compare to similar businesses

You don’t need to list every detail, just give a clear idea of what you offer and how you’ll charge for it.
