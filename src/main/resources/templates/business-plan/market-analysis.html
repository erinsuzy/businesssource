Market Analysis

Describe the market for your business. You don’t need formal data — just thoughtful answers to questions like:

  • Who are your ideal customers?
  • What problems do they face that your business solves?
  • Are there similar businesses in your area?
  • What sets your business apart from the competition?
  • Is the demand for your service or product growing?

If you're not sure, think about your own experiences, conversations you've had, or what you’ve seen online or locally.
